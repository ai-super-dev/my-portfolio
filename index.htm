Webflow

What is Webflow?

Webflow is an all-in-one web design and development platform that allows designers and developers to build websites.

Why is Webflow good for me?

Time saving: Webflow offers an easy and quick way to create websites without having to have large review loops with programming and designers.

Power and speed: Webflow offers a fast and powerful hosting platform based on modern technologies and proven methods. This allows me to provide my customers with a website with excellent performance and fast load times.
‍
Scalability: Webflow provides built-in e-commerce features that enable us to offer our customers scalable e-commerce solutions that can grow with your business.

‍Design flexibility: With Webflow, I can quickly create or modify a variety of designs. This gives me the freedom to build websites that meet my clients' needs and requirements.

How long does it take to build a website?

The time it takes to have a website created by us depends on individual needs and requirements. There is no general time specification here. It depends on the number of pages as well as the number of sections. How much animation and design work is required varies greatly.

As long as we have all graphics, texts and information, we can deliver a landing page (one-pager) with 6 sections within 2 days. Most of the time, however, you still need an imprint/privacy policy and cookie integrations to bring a full-fledged website online.

Do you also offer website maintenance and support?

Yes, if you subscribe, we offer ongoing support and maintenance for the website to ensure that it remains effective and secure. With the “editor”, it is possible to do simple things yourself, such as changing texts or exchanging images, to increase effectiveness.

Can you also create an e-commerce website?

Yes, we can also create shop websites. We offer a wide range of services, including the creation of online shops on and via Webflow.

Do you also offer training for Webflow?

Yes, we also offer training for Webflow. We can provide training for individuals or groups to ensure that you can use the platform effectively. Please book a telephone call in this regard to clarify further details with us.

Can we also hire you to update our existing website?

Maybe! It depends on which systems, platforms or the same you work with.
Prices & subscription

How does the pause function work?

We understand that you might not have enough design work to fill an entire month. Maybe you only have one or two design requests right now. That's where pausing your subscription comes in. The billing cycles are based on a period of one month, i.e. 30 days. Let's say you sign up and use the service for 20 days and then decide to pause your subscription. This means that the billing cycle is paused and you still have 10 days of service left, which you can use at any time in the future. For the break, no project should be “in progress” or just wait “for approval.”

Is there a refund if I don't like the service?

Due to the high quality of work and the all-inclusive service, no refunds are given.
